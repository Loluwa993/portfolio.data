SQL: International Breweries Sales Analysis

Objective: To analyze International Breweries products level of consumption (sales) across different areas (continent, regions) and support decision-making for promotional targeting.

üí°Example of Business Question Answered:
Which regions in Nigeria had the highest Budweiser consumption in 2019, and what percentage of the total did each contribute?

üß† My Approach:

  • Calculate total consumption in Nigeria for Budweiser in 2019
  • Use CTE for storing total consumption
  • Aggregate consumption per region
  • Calculate percentage contribution by region
  • Order regions by descending percentage

üîç SQL Code

								WITH Ttl_Qty_Bud_Cons_in_Nig_2019 AS 
(SELECT SUM(intrnlbrew.quantity) AS Ttl_Qty_Bud_Cons_in_Nig_2019
FROM intrnlbrew
WHERE Brands = 'budweiser' AND Countries = 'Nigeria' AND years = '2019')

SELECT Region, SUM(intrnlbrew.quantity) AS Qty_Bud_Cons_per_Region_Nig_2019,
(SUM(intrnlbrew.quantity) * 100.0) / Ttl_Qty_Bud_Cons_in_Nig_2019 AS Ptge_Bud_Cons_per_Region_Nig_2019
FROM intrnlbrew, Ttl_Qty_Bud_Cons_in_Nig_2019
WHERE Brands = 'budweiser' AND Countries = 'Nigeria' AND years = '2019'
GROUP BY Region, Ttl_Qty_Bud_Cons_in_Nig_2019
ORDER BY Ptge_Bud_Cons_per_Region_Nig_2019 DESC;

This analysis enables the marketing team to identify high-performing regions and target promotional campaigns more effectively. It also highlights potential growth areas in underrepresented regions.
